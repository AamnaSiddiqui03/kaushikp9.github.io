ABOUT STEP UP

STEPUP 5.0

Step Up Event is an annual 5k run organised by RSC, under the direction of the RNSIT Physical Education Department. The event has different themes and purposes every year, but the goal is always the same - to promote physical activity, community involvement, and social responsibility. The fifth iteration of Step up will be held on June 11, 2023 .By organising a 5k run with the theme of planting trees, we can raise funds and awareness about how planting saplings is crucial for mitigating climate change, conserving biodiversity, and promoting soil conservation. Therefore, it is essential to increase awareness of the benefits of planting trees and encourage individuals and communities to participate in tree-planting activities.

STEPUP 4.0

The themes of the previous events ranged from promoting environmental awareness, to supporting charitable organisations, to celebrating cultural diversity. And now, it has become a tradition for RNSIT to organise Step Up every year through RSC on the theme “Go Green”, and has inspired many individuals to take up running and other forms of exercise. During the race, medals and certificates are bestowed to all the participants. The event has also raised funds for various purposes and causes. Last year, Step up 4.0 was based on the theme “you run, we plant”. Accordingly, for each registration, a sapling was planted and a donation certificate was provided to the participants. The focus of this year’s race remains the same, - “You run, We plant” and the funds collected during this event will be used to support this cause.
